Why join NEWAY?

Transparency
Transparency is the main value of NEWAY. Not only does blockchain technology enables to see every transaction of NEW. The NEWAY team commited to be as transparent as possible to create the best possible community and atmosphere.
Save money
Blockchain technology comes with no transaction costs. Admittely, it is not free to exchange NEW against other currencies. However, after having bought NEW there will be no more costs. In addtion, a guaranteed discount when paying with NEW will more than cover these costs.
Fairness
Being a part of the NEWAY community doesn't only mean that you will save money but it will also allow you to help sustainable businesses to develop. You will be awarded not only with NEW tokens but also by making the world a better place!
